# Contract Terms and Renewal Policy

## Standard Contract Terms

### Contract Duration
- Default contract term: 12 months
- Auto-renewal: Enabled by default
- Renewal notification: 30 days prior to renewal date

### Early Termination
- Termination notice: 30 days written notice required
- Early termination fee: 50% of remaining contract value
- Exception: No fee if service issues documented

### Renewal Process
1. Automatic renewal unless notice provided
2. Pricing locked for renewal period
3. Option to upgrade/downgrade at renewal
4. New features included automatically

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel
- Email: support@company.com
- Portal: Account settings > Cancel subscription
- Phone: Call support team

### Effective Date
- Cancellation effective at end of current billing period
- No partial month refunds
- Access continues until period end

### Data Retention
- 90 days after cancellation
- Export available during retention period
- Permanent deletion after 90 days

## Contract Modifications

### Mid-Contract Changes
- Upgrades: Effective immediately, pro-rated billing
- Downgrades: Effective at next renewal
- User count changes: Adjusted in next billing cycle

### Price Changes
- 60 days notice for price increases
- Option to cancel without penalty during notice period
- Grandfathered pricing for existing customers (12 months)

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)

### Uptime Guarantee
- Standard: 99.5% uptime
- Professional: 99.9% uptime  
- Enterprise: 99.99% uptime with custom SLA

### Credits for Downtime
- Below 99.5%: 10% monthly credit
- Below 99%: 25% monthly credit
- Below 95%: 50% monthly credit

## Legal Terms

### Governing Law
- Jurisdiction: Delaware, USA
- Dispute resolution: Binding arbitration
- Class action waiver: Included

### Data Privacy
- GDPR compliant
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- Data processing agreement available

### Intellectual Property
- Customer retains all rights to their data
- Company retains rights to platform
- No license to customer content beyond service provision
